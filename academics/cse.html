Department of Computer Science and Engineering

About Department

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering was established in 2012. The department has state-of-the-art computing facilities and experienced faculty members dedicated to producing industry-ready software professionals.

Vision

  • To be a center of excellence in Computer Science and Engineering education, fostering innovation and producing socially responsible engineers.

Mission

  • To provide quality education in theoretical and practical aspects of Computer Science
  • To promote research and innovation in emerging technologies
  • To develop industry-ready professionals with strong ethical values
  • To foster entrepreneurship and leadership qualities

Laboratories

  • Programming Laboratory
  • Data Structures Laboratory
  • Database Management Systems Lab
  • Networks Laboratory
  • Web Technology Laboratory
  • Project Laboratory
