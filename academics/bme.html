Department of Bio-Medical Engineering

About Department

The biomedical department was launched in the year 2020-21 with full sanctioned strength of 60 students. The vision of the department is to impart quality education and provide enhanced knowledge to the society. The department aims to be nationally recognised institute for undergraduate engineering studies. The department is equipped with good infrastructure and possess many laboratories related with biomedical engineering. The department has many experienced faculties to deliver and to provide cutting edge technology to the students. The department has planned to establish a centre of excellence lab for Biomedical engineering. The department aim is to develop clinically translatable medical solutions for human health by training the next generation of Bio,medical engineers.

Vision

  • To develop an innovative and multi-disciplinary skills relating the fundamentals of Biomedical engineering with other disciplines to improve the health care and to increase life expectancy.

Mission

  • To enrich the learning skills and to emphasize the usage of technical knowledge to improve the quality and betterment of life
  • To practice excellence in Biomedical Engineering by relating engineering and medicine through education

Laboratories

  • Fundamentals Of Electronic And Components LAB
  • Sensor And Measurements Lab
  • Digital Electronics Lab
  • Embedded And IOMT Lab
  • Biomedical Instruments Lab
  • Diagnostics And Therapeutic Lab
  • Analog And Circuit Lab
