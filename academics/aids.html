Department of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science

About Department

The Artificial Intelligence and Data Science department were founded in 2022 with one undergraduate program affiliated with Anna University, approved by AICTE. This course aims to provide the core technologies such as artificial intelligence, data mining, and data modelling and give intensive inputs in machine learning and big data analytics. By the end of this course, the students will gain cross-disciplinary skills across fields such as statistics, computer science, machine learning, and logic, data scientists and will have career opportunities in healthcare, business, eCommerce, social networking companies, climatology, biotechnology, genetics, and other essential areas. The central focus of this programme is to equip students with statistical, mathematical reasoning, machine learning, knowledge discovery, and visualization skills.

Vision

  • To promote ethical guidelines and standards within the AI community and advocates for the integration of ethical considerations into AI research and practice.
  • To harness the power of AI to address some of the most pressing societal challenges, such as healthcare, education, environmental sustainability, poverty alleviation, and social justice.
  • To foster collaboration and knowledge exchange, the department seeks to leverage diverse perspectives and expertise to advance AI research and applications effectively.

Mission

  • Equip individuals with the skills and knowledge needed to work with AI effectively and responsibly.
  • Seeks partnerships with academia, industry, government agencies, non-profit organizations, and other stakeholders to exchange knowledge, share resources, and tackle complex challenges collaboratively.
  • Supports innovation, protect privacy and security, ensure fairness and transparency, and promote the responsible use of AI technology.
