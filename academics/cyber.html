Department of Computer Science and Engineering (Cyber Security)

About Department

The CSE-Cyber Security department was founded in 2023 with one undergraduate program affiliated with Anna University, approved by AICTE. The programme aims at producing high quality software professionals for industry through sound theoretical and practical training. The Department today stands out as a beacon to all, in the field of Computer education and the seed that was planted sixteen years back is now a path leader. It is with great satisfaction that the department looks back to the tireless struggle of the past and to the many peaks to be conquered in the future.

Vision

  • Empower and support young learners in creating a sustainable, compassionate, and research-focused educational platform in cyber security to foster a secure, resilient, and threat-free digital world.

Mission

  • Enable the adoption of Cyber and Information Security best practices, seamlessly integrating them into organizational workflows, empowering businesses to proactively manage and mitigate risks, and enhancing their global competitiveness.
  • Inspire young minds to educate and empower society in restoring nationwide human safety and security within the digital realm.
  • Establish a project-oriented learning environment centered around collaboration and teamwork, aimed at tackling global challenges while upholding human and ethical values
