Department of Agriculture Engineering

About Department

Department of Agriculture Engineering, established in the year 2018, offers 4 years B.E Degree in Agriculture Engineering course. This department is equipped with state of the art laboratory facilities with highly qualified Members of Faculty. Agricultural processing laboratory has been built to serve the demands of teaching and research on the Processing Technology of agriculture produces. To impart the knowledge on crop cultivation aspects from seed to harvest, the department has a 3 acres' farm. The department offers hands-on training for students from first year onwards on the latest agricultural engineering technologies.

Vision

  • To create agricultural engineers with a wealth of knowledge who can satisfy the demand for their abilities nationally and serve society to attain food and nutritional security.

Mission

  • To facilitate lifelong learning by providing agriculturally focused academic programs.
  • To promote agrarian reforms through research based agricultural policy
  • To disseminate knowledge to the community through extension and out reach
