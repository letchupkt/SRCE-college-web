About SRCE

Sri Ramakrishna College of Engineering (SRCE) is a premier educational institution established in 2009 under Sri Swamy Vivekananda Educational Trust. Located in Perambalur, Tamil Nadu, SRCE has emerged as a center of excellence in engineering education.

Academic Excellence

AICTE approved programs with experienced faculty and modern teaching methodologies.

State-of-the-art Infrastructure

Well-equipped laboratories, digital library, and modern amenities for hands-on learning.

Industry Connect

Strong industry partnerships ensuring excellent placement opportunities for students.

Our Vision

"To be a premier institution offering quality technical education and creating innovative engineers with social responsibility."

Our Mission

"To educate students with technical knowledge, professional skills, and ethical values, preparing them to be innovative leaders in the global society."

Chairman's Message

A small middle school, whose foundation was laid in the year 1990 has grown into a Group of Institutions. The founder of the college, Dr.M.Sivasubramaniam, started Sri Swami Vivekananda Educational Trust in the year 1990.
