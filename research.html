Research

About Research

Sri Ramakrishna College of Engineering has taken concerted efforts towards differentiating itself by encouraging innovation and research. This Institution provides support and guidance to enable faculty and students to take up research and innovative development relevant to industry as well as society. The dedicated research teams add its strength and spreads its roots over diverse areas like big data, intelligence systems, cloud computing, CAD/CAM, Information Technology, Knowledge Management, Electronics and Communication, Bio medical, Agricultural etc. Having realized a dire scope and demand of research in the emerging fields, these key ultra-tech disciplines are focused to bring out the desired results for the betterment of the society.

Objectives

To motivate faculty and students to do research to address the industry and societal problems. To encourage the faculty and students to do the projects in innovative way and find the new technology which leads to the development of entrepreneur or transfer the technology to the industry. The research outcomes may be published in reputed journals.
