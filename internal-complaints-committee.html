Internal Complaints Committee

An Internal Complaints Cell (ICC), also known as an Internal Complaints Committee (ICC) or Internal Grievance Redressal Cell, is a dedicated unit within an organization or institution tasked with handling complaints related to harassment, discrimination, or any form of misconduct within the workplace or institution. The primary functions and responsibilities of an Internal Complaints Cell typically include

  • Receiving Complaints
  • Investigation and Resolution
  • Mediation and Counseling
  • Awareness and Prevention
  • Monitoring and Evaluation
